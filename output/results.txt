SkyPredict: Explainable Flight Delay Monitoring Platform
-------------------------------------------------------

Test Environment:
- Platform: Localhost (Flask)
- URL: http://127.0.0.1:5000
- ML Model: Random Forest Classifier
- Dataset: flight_data.csv
- Prediction Type: Probability-based risk scoring

-------------------------------------------------------
Sample Test Case 1
-------------------------------------------------------
Input Parameters:
Airline       : IndiGo
Origin Airport: DEL
Destination   : BOM
Day           : 15
Month         : 6
Weekday       : Tuesday
Departure Hour: 18

Prediction Output:
Delay Risk Score : 78.42 %
Risk Category    : High Risk

Explainable AI (Top Factors):
1. DepHour       – Peak hour congestion impact
2. Airline       – Operational delay patterns
3. Destination  – Airport traffic density

Overall Observations
-------------------------------------------------------
- The system successfully predicts delay risk as a percentage.
- Risk categorization improves decision-making clarity.
- Explainable AI increases trust by identifying top contributing factors.
- Peak-hour departures show higher delay probability.
- Airline and destination significantly influence delay risk.

Conclusion
-------------------------------------------------------
The SkyPredict system demonstrates effective integration of machine learning,
probability-based risk assessment, and explainable AI to support informed
